Tech Innovations for Social Impact

Being a firm believer in the values of a social enterprise, Anu enjoys working with Entrepreneurs and SMEs essentially to promote their tech innovation led businesses – by imbuing elements that would connect the business goals with social impact. Her interests are centered around working with leadership, founding teams and VCs in building tech innovation strategies that would ultimately provide for impact. ANURADHA BISWAS

Talking about Anu would also essentially mean talking about her brain child Prakat. Driven by learning and mentorship from great industry leaders such as the founders of Infosys she has achieved in building a successful company over the last 10 years with new growth avenues in every passing year. She has now built a company that employs 120+ professionals in India and the US, which is a company that also carries with it a strong social vision – which is to create jobs and foster professional opportunities for persons with disabilities.

Deeply inspired at a very early age by her grandfather who was one of the most senior most administrators in the royal courts of erstwhile Mysore state, she was exposed to ideas of nation building, economic development, woman empowerment and the binding link between all of these and social inclusion. All of these culminated into the seed of the idea of a company with a social vision focused on high professionalism and diversity.

With technology as the backbone of her career, Anu started Prakat solutions, that designed, developed and deployed Technology products that embodied principles of Quality Engineering & Software Engineering. The company has today evolved to be a Technology innovations company, focused on solving problems that impact businesses and their users worldwide.

Anu strongly believes in sharing her own life lessons and experiences to help others in achieving their objectives and support efforts leading to them. She is a firm believer in the power of entrepreneurs & women leaders committed to helping aspiring new generation of leaders to reach their full potential. Her renewed goal in life is to help build a sustainable future for all.
