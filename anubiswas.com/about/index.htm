In Debt.

Living paycheck to paycheck.

Feeling like an imposter.

I’ve been there

I started out making 20k/year and getting into almost as much in credit card debt. What was ironic was that I took a job as a finance anchor at the time… but I was a mess with my own. I eventually got out of this mess, but when I finally did, I learned a valuable lesson.

We've all heard the cliché...

Money doesn't buy happiness.

But I'm here to tell you...

Successfully managing it

Can.

I wanted to share my experience and show the world how.

Through my stories.

Once my book was published, I learned...

My decisions.

My mistakes.

Mattered.

None of us learns any of this in school. The people who really needed me weren't the already rich dudes who watch finance news, but my former self. The broke girl. The lost girl.

I was so surprised and grateful to learn that my book helped so many people like myself.

And my book became a bestseller.

This might be the new chapter in your life.

Fill it only with the

Best decisions.

I can help.

In life and in finances, it’s better to beat low expectations. I know what it’s like to be broke. To be in debt. I got out of there and I’m not going back. And I’ll tell you exactly how I did it…and If I could do it, anyone can.
